About Us

Augmont Goldtech Private Limited is a consumer retail Goldtech company coming from the same group as Augmont Enterprises Private Limited- India’s largest and completely integrated gold company-from refining to retailing.

Augmont:

The word Augmont is a combination of Au and Ag, the chemical symbols for 'Gold' and 'Silver' respectively and the word “Augment” that means to increase or to make something greater by adding to it. This is a perfect embodiment of Augmont's philosophy of improving and adding value to the entire ecosystem of precious metals.

Augmont today has become almost synonymous with gold in India and millions of jewellers find us as their preferred partner.

Augmont’s innovative DNA makes it our responsibility to revolutionize the entire gold ecosystem and help people transform their dreams into reality via easy access to gold.

Hence the need to launch Augmont Goldtech Private Limited.

Augmont Goldtech is into ‘Phygital Gold Retailing’ where consumers can avail innovative, trusted, and useful gold products -all under one roof and one app.

Brand 'Gold For All' :

Augmont "GOLD FOR ALL" is a revolutionary Goldtech ecosystem to make Gold accessible, affordable, useful, and manageable for all phases of life. It is a one-stop destination for Gold.

SIGNIFICANCE

Augmont as a brand has been carefully derived on the basis of what it does best – gold and silver related online trading, refining and manufacturing. It is a company that is positive in its attitude and has always looked towards progression and growth.

  • A G : Silver is a metallic element with symbol Ag (from Latin: argentum)

2.A U G M O N T :

Augmont has been derived from the word ‘augment’ which means ‘make (something) greater by adding to it’ This perfectly matches with Augmont’s philosophy, work and what it does – refining impure gold to pure gold (improving) and jewellery manufacturing (adding value).
